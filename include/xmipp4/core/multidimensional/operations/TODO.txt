Reductions (whole-array or along an axis)
	- sum, prod
	- min, max  (and their argument variants: argmin, argmax)

	- sumproduct (dot product of two arrays), euclidean_distance, euclidean_distance2
Masked reductions
	- same set as plain reductions, but accepting a boolean mask array
